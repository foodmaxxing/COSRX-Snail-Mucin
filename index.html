The Ingredient

What Is Snail Mucin, Actually?

Let's skip the squeamishness and get to the science — because once you understand what this ingredient does, the price tag makes a lot more sense.

Snail mucin is the secretion produced by land snails, collected humanely and used as a cosmetic active. In nature, snails produce it to protect and heal their own skin from cuts, UV damage, and dehydration. When applied to human skin, the same mechanisms kick in.

The key components in snail mucin that make it effective are: glycoproteins (which help skin cells communicate and repair), hyaluronic acid (a heavyweight humectant that holds water in the skin), glycolic acid (a mild exfoliant that improves texture), zinc (anti-inflammatory and pore-minimizing), and allantoin (a soothing agent that speeds up cell turnover).

The COSRX formula specifically uses 96.3% Snail Secretion Filtrate — meaning the vast majority of what you're putting on your face is the active ingredient, not filler, fragrance, or alcohol.

The ingredient list is refreshingly short — 96.3% is the star, and the supporting cast is minimal and gentle.

The Routine

How to Use It — The Right Way

Where you place this in your routine matters. Essences go after watery steps and before thicker moisturizers — here's exactly how to layer it.

The essence has a slightly sticky texture when first applied — that dissipates within 30–60 seconds. Pat, don't rub.
1
Cleanser

Start with a gentle, pH-balanced cleanser. Avoid anything stripping — it counteracts what the essence is about to do.

2
Toner (Optional)

If you use a hydrating toner, apply it first. It boosts absorption for everything that follows.

3

Dispense 2–3 pumps, warm between fingers, then gently press and pat into skin. Don't rub. Let it absorb for 30–60 seconds — the slight tackiness will disappear.

4

Vitamin C, niacinamide, or other serums go on next. Lightest texture first.

5
Moisturizer

Seal everything in with a moisturizer suited to your skin type. This locks in the humectants the essence just deposited.

6
SPF (AM only)

Always finish your morning routine with a broad-spectrum SPF. Your skin barrier work is wasted without sun protection.

Use AM and PM for best results. Most people see a meaningful difference within 2–4 weeks of consistent daily use.
